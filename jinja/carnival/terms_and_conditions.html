PENNYLANE QUANTUM CARNIVAL

October 29, 2021

Description

The PennyLane Quantum Carnival (the “Competition”) consists of multiple opportunities to win tickets (points) over the course of the month of November (2021) based upon initial provision of a participant’s email. ONE activity requires specific entrance requirements with judging by the Organizer. At the end of the month, based upon total points collected, allowable prize(s) (each an “Award”) will be chosen by the participants and distributed.

Terms and conditions applicable to each Award are detailed in the section labelled “Awards” below. In addition, all awards are covered by the Competition General Rules and Regulations.

Organizer

The organizer of this Competition is Xanadu Quantum Technologies Inc., 777 Bay St., Toronto ON, M5G 2C8, Canada (“Xanadu”, “we”, or “us”).

Competition Period

The Competition will commence when Xanadu begins accepting email submissions through pennylane.ai/carnival/index.html. This will start taking place at approximately 9:00 am ET 1 November 2021. The Competition end date is 5:59 pm ET 30 November 2021.

Awards

Talent Show Award
  • Total Award(s): 30 Points (to the winner)
  • Description: Participants submit their drawings, memes, videos, music, or other creative work that promote PennyLane, coding, quantum computers, or other related topics.
  • Rules:
    • Entrants and Submissions must conform to the General Rules and Regulations.
    • Submissions must be submitted onto an Organizer-provider link along with the participant’s email address.
    • Multiple submissions can be made, but only one ticket will be awarded (rather than tickets for each individual piece).
  • Award criteria: The three grading criteria will be creativity, pertinence, and quality. The participant with the highest grade overall will get 30 tickets. Second place will win 20 tickets. Third prize will win 10 tickets. Others with outstanding submissions (at least 2 points per grading criterion) that don’t make the top 3 can win 5 tickets per participant. All participants will get 1 ticket for making a submission to the competition.
  • Judging criteria: Two Organizers will provide a grade on a scale from 1-3 for each criterion and for each submission.
  • Award entry period: 1 November 2021, ending 22 November 2021 (5:59 PM ET).
  • Other considerations: Submissions may be profiled on Xanadu’s social media, at Xanadu’s discretion. Submissions may be posted/used on Xanadu’s sites (e.g., incorporated into the PennyLane webpage or displayed on xanadu.ai).

PennyLane Scavenger Hunt Final Award(s)

  • Prize(s): A variety of Xanadu-themed and other miscellaneous products.
  • Description: Participants enrol/register/participate in as many Scavenger Hunt activities offered by the Organizer as they would like over the course of the month. Tickets will be awarded for participating in each event. Participants can discover additional tickets hidden on the internet.
  • Rules:
    • Entrants and Submissions must conform to the General Rules and Regulations.
    • Tickets accumulated through the various activities will award a different number of points according to their colour; events will give more points according to the level of difficulty associated in earning those points.
    • All participants must submit the email address they registered for the scavenger hunt, their first and last name (optional) and up to 25 unique ticket codes.
    • Participants may update their submission before the competition closing; however, only the last submission of tickets from each participant will be considered.
  • Award criteria: Participants can convert their accumulated points into prizes based upon the following levels:
    • Up to 50 participants will receive prizes in the form of a t-shirt, hat, notebook, stickers or similar items.
    • Participants who have gathered at least 23 points are eligible to receive the prizes mentioned.
    • The participants with the most number of points in their final submission will have priority in the swag allocation.
    • In case of a tie for the 50th place the tie-breaker will be the date of submission of the code submission form, where the person with the earliest final submission of the form will get the last prize available.
  • Award entry period: Participants must submit their gathered tickets before 5:59 PM Eastern Time 30 November 2021. The unique codes for submitted tickets will be validated and points summed for each participant by the Organizer. Xanadu will notify participants of their total points by December 15, 2021 and participants will have 60 days with which to redeem their selected prizes as outlined above.
  • Other considerations: Participants may be profiled on Xanadu’s social media platforms and/or website.
